Section: SEBI Guidelines - Insider Trading

As per SEBI (Prohibition of Insider Trading) Regulations, 2015:
- Insiders are prohibited from trading in securities while in possession of unpublished price sensitive information (UPSI).
- Companies must maintain a structured digital database of persons with whom UPSI is shared.
- Disclosure of trades by promoters, employees, and directors must be made within 7 working days.

Section: RBI Circular - KYC Norms

The Reserve Bank of India mandates:
- Customer identification must be done using Aadhaar or PAN.
- Banks must conduct periodic KYC updates, with high-risk customers requiring annual reviews.
- Suspicious transactions must be reported to the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU-IND) promptly.

Section: FEMA - Capital Account Transactions

Under the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), 1999:
- All capital account transactions require prior approval from RBI unless specifically exempted.
- Investments by NRIs must comply with sectoral caps and reporting timelines.
- Any contravention may attract penalties under Section 13 of FEMA.
